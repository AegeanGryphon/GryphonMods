About

A better experience,
by the community

LumenTale is a game worth loving deeper. GryphonMods started as a personal project to fix small frustrations and grew into a curated collection of enhancements that make a good game even better.

Every mod here is hand-crafted, thoroughly tested against the live game, and designed to feel like something the developers could have shipped themselves — no game-breaking cheats, no spoilers, just thoughtful improvements.

🛠️

Curated & Maintained

Every mod is hand-built and kept up to date as the game patches. Quality over quantity.

🚀

One-Click Launcher

The GryphonMods Launcher installs BepInEx, manages your mods, and keeps everything updated automatically.

🐛

Bug Fixes Included

Some mods exist purely to patch vanilla bugs the developers haven't addressed yet — no EXP glitches, no broken quests.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

The launcher handles all the technical setup so you can focus on playing.

1

Download Launcher

Grab the latest release from GitHub. One small .exe — no installer required.

2

Detect Your Game

The launcher finds LumenTale via Steam automatically, or you can point it manually.

3

Install BepInEx

One click sets up the mod framework. The launcher handles the whole process.

4

Pick Your Mods

Browse, install, and update mods from directly within the launcher. Done.
